Use this as a starting map; always open and cite the current rule text from the Utah Administrative Rules site.

Source for the headings below: Utah DEQ "Air Quality Laws and Rules" page.

- **Series 400**: Permits: New and Modified Sources
- **Series 500**: Air Pollution Control Programs
- **Series 800**: Compliance, Enforcement, and Liability

## Note on "Series 600-700"

The DEQ landing page may not list headings for every historical/legacy series. If a user asks about "Series 600" or "Series 700" specifically:

1. Use the Utah Administrative Rules site's index/search for `R307-6*` or `R307-7*`.
2. Confirm whether the series exists, has been repealed, or has been renumbered.
3. Answer with the current citation and effective date/history.
