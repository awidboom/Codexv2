### Working reserve view

If the goal is a practical reserve for items still lacking an assigned penalty:
- Unresolved NOVs only: around $350,000 to $375,000, with a reasonable downside floor near $250,000 and upside case near $525,000.
- Unresolved NOVs plus 2025 self-disclosed deviations: around $440,000 to $455,000, with a broader range of about $307,500 to $675,000.

### Main uncertainty drivers

- Whether P80830 is treated as one heavily discounted grouped case or unpacked into several independently penalized components.
- Whether SCAQMD gives credit for downtime, rebuild periods, prompt reporting, and corrective actions.
- Whether repeated 2025 pH / flow deviations are viewed as routine monitoring drift or as repeat noncompliance after prior NOV history.
- Whether P69321 contains facts stronger than the limited timeline summary suggests.
