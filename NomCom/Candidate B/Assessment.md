Overall, the evaluation form narrative appears broadly consistent with the supporting materials in the folder on: (1) Sarah’s strength as a schedule-driven PM who can lead multi-disciplinary teams, (2) visible progress on PIC-readiness/visibility based on the officer pulse survey swing from 11/2024 to 11/2025, (3) a coherent development theme around “operating more as a PIC” (delegation, staying out of the weeds, strategic framing), and (4) coaching/mentoring impact.

- Some quantified claims (e.g., proposal counts/success rates) are not corroborated elsewhere in the folder and should be treated as “evaluation-form only” unless NomCom confirms a source of record.
