Nominating Committee Duties
Briefly the Nominating Committee’s duties are:
•	Attend annual meeting of candidate reports (approximately 45 – 60 minute meetings per candidate) 
•	Determine if candidates recommended by coaching committees should be presented to the whole BU officer group for nomination as officer in 2026
•	Provide feedback to the coaching committees and Facilitator 
•	Perform edge-matching between candidates within BU 
•	Provide actionable feedback for candidates for continued development as appropriate 
Nominating Committee Activities
You should have three meetings between 2/3 and 2/4 where the officer candidate coaching committees will report on the progress of the candidate and provide a recommendation. The recommendations of the coaching team would be:
•	Continue coaching for the next year;
•	Nominate the candidate to the entire EM Officer group; or
•	Remove candidate from the pipeline.
The candidate coaching committee will be providing a draft long form for the candidate to the Nominating Committee ahead of the February 2026 Nominating Committee annual meetings. Please plan on spending time ahead of the meeting to review the draft long forms. We are targeting getting the draft long forms out to you by January 22.

After the Nominating Committee annual meeting, you will meet again to compare notes, evaluate each candidate, and prepare the Nominating Committee’s recommendations for each candidate. It is the Officer Process Coordinator’s responsibility to schedule the annual meetings, take notes during the coaching committee’s presentations, document the recommendations and basis of the Nominating Committee’s recommendation, and present the information to each candidate’s coaching committee.
