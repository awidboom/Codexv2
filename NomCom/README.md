# NomCom (2026 EM Officer Nominating Committee)

This folder contains working materials for my participation as a member of the 2026 EM Officer Nominating Committee.

## Objective

My goal is to evaluate each officer candidate against their evaluation form and provide clear, actionable feedback and a recommendation as part of the Nominating Committee process.

## Evaluation Basis

The candidate evaluation form is informed by:

- `officer charter and supporting attachments.docx` (officer expectations and selection criteria)
- `Principal Vice President role description.docx` (role-specific expectations and responsibilities)

These documents provide the detailed criteria and examples used to interpret and apply the evaluation form consistently across candidates.

## Notes on Evidence Expectations

- It is **not** the expectation that candidates complete contracting activities without a PIC, or that risk calls were made without PIC involvement. Candidate materials may appropriately describe supporting, contributing to, or learning from PIC-led contracting and risk decisions.
- For leverage (percent of work performed by the PM), an approximate target range is **~20–35%**. More leverage (lower PM %) can be appropriate with the right circumstances and a senior team; less leverage (higher PM %) can be appropriate in more technical roles.

## Supporting Process References

- `Nominating Committee Duties.docx` (committee responsibilities and annual review expectations)
- `Process for developing and appointing business unit vice presidents.pdf` (process flow, roles, and schedule context)
- `Kickoff Email.txt` (kickoff notes, candidate list, and key dates)

## Folder Structure

- `Candidate A/`, `Candidate B/`, `Candidate C/`: candidate-specific working materials (e.g., long forms, notes, completed evaluations).
