### LFAR-based claims - mostly consistent, but require careful wording

Two statements in the narrative are directionally supported, but depend on how the LFAR is summarized:
- "Well leveraged / <30% worked by PM": supported when weighted by hours managed (and also true for the two largest managed-hour clients), but not true if interpreted as a simple per-row threshold or weighted by hours worked.

Recommendation: in the final materials, specify the metric (e.g., "weighted by hours managed") and avoid absolute language like "consistently" unless it is demonstrably true under the intended calculation.
