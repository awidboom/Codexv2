The Nominating Committee is in agreement with the coaching team’s recommendation not to nominate Beau as a PIC in 2025.
Beau has made good progress towards the development goals identified in his long form, and upon review, the Nominating Committee recommends additional development related to adjusting style when the circumstance calls for consensus building and patience. His confidence is a strength but has the potential to be an overused strength. Further, his writing and communication in certain circumstances (for example, when communicating to client company leadership or attorneys) can benefit from a greater amount of explanatory preamble, rather than quickly arriving at conclusions which may seem self-evident to technical professionals. During the Coaching Committee’s presentation to the Nominating Committee, business development and strategic pursuits were also noted as potential development areas not currently addressed in the long form.
We understand that Beau will be afforded key opportunities to enhance his skills relative to Criteria A2 and A3 as the “face of Barr” to enhance his skills in trusted advisor communications (e.g., do not immediately assume you know what the client is asking for, and do not immediately jump to an answer, but instead build collaboration on the issue) and assuring high-quality deliverables for:
HF Sinclair (via advanced tanks emissions compliance management system),
Scranton Holding (via permitting), and
Monroe Energy (via routine environmental compliance support).
