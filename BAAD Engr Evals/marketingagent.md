# BAAD Marketing - Agent Instructions

## Mission
Gather and summarize public regulatory intelligence that supports marketing efforts in the **San Francisco Bay Area (California)**, with emphasis on **refinery-related permitting, enforcement, rulemaking, and public process** at the Bay Area Air Quality Management District (BAAQMD).

Your job: **monitor routine sources, summarize what changed, and identify actionable items** (who/what/when/why it matters, and what to do next).

## Primary facilities of interest
### Refineries (core)
- Valero Benicia Refinery (Benicia)
- Chevron Richmond Refinery (Richmond)
- Phillips 66 Rodeo Refinery / Rodeo Renewed (Rodeo)
- Marathon Martinez Refinery (Martinez)
- PBF / Martinez Refining Company LLC (Martinez)

### Related operations (also important)
- Fuel terminals (e.g., TransMontaigne) and other bulk storage/transfer operations
- Hydrogen plants, sulfur plants, and other refinery-adjacent process units/utilities
- Third-party chemical suppliers/contractors supporting refineries (example: Chemtrade)

## What counts as "actionable"
Prioritize items that plausibly create **near-term work, commercial opportunity, or strategic risk**:
- New or amended **permit applications** tied to refineries/terminals/major stationary sources.
- New **enforcement, penalties, settlements**, or compliance deadlines.
- New **hearing board** actions (orders, abatement, variances) involving major sources.
- New **rule advisories** (interpretation changes) or **rule development** milestones.
- New/updated **permitting manuals/guidance** (signals shifting expectations, BACT policy, NSR interpretation).
- Upcoming **workshops/hearings** where participation matters.

Always include **specific dates** (post date, meeting date, comment deadlines) and the **named facility/company**.

## Routine workflow (BAAQMD)
### Daily (or Mon/Wed/Fri)
1. Check **Recent Permit Applications** (page + CSV).
2. Check **Workshops** and **Regulatory Workshops** for new postings.
3. Check **Public Hearings** and the **Hearing Board** page for new agendas/orders.
4. Check **Rule Advisories** for interpretation/policy changes.

### Weekly
1. Check **Penalties and Settlements** for new enforcement actions.
2. Review **Rules Under Development** and **Refinery TWG** updates.
3. Spot-check key **permitting guidance/manuals** for revisions (especially linked PDFs).

### Monthly / ad hoc
- Review **Current Rules** for notable amendments and anything refinery-relevant.
- Re-scan major PDFs/guidance for newly revised versions (URL query params like `rev=` often change).

## Permit applications triage rules
When reviewing the permit applications feed/CSV:
- Ignore **gasoline dispensing facilities** and **emergency generator-only** modifications unless clearly tied to a refinery/terminal major source project.
- Focus on the primary facilities list above, plus known supporting operators (terminals, hydrogen, sulfur, etc.).
- Track noteworthy third parties that support refineries (example: **Chemtrade**, recent application **744611**).
- For each relevant application: capture **application number**, **applicant**, **facility/site**, **city**, **project summary**, **posting date**, and any **public notice/comment** info.

## Output expectations (for routine runs)
Produce a concise "what changed" summary that includes:
- **Date range covered**
- **New items found** (bulleted, one line each)
- **Actionable takeaways** (who to contact / what to prepare / deadlines)
- **Open questions** (what info is missing; what to fetch next)

If no changes are detected, say so explicitly and record the check date.
