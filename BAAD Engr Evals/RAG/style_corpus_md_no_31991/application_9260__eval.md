STATEMENT OF COMPLIANCE
ANCE 
 
The owner/operator of S-1491 Fixed Volume Portable Tank is subject to Regulation 8-5-301 Storage Tank Control Requirements.  For tanks greater than 75 m3 (19,803 gallons) but less than 150 m3 (39,626 gallons) with organic contents with a true vapor pressure greater or equal to 11 psia, a pressure tank or approved emission control system 
is required.  The owner/operator of S-1491 is subject to Regulation 8-5-306 Requirements for Approved Emission 
Control Systems.  The owner/opera tor will be required to abate S-1491 with A-1001 Carbon Canister and A-1002 
Carbon Canister at all times.  The canisters must be gas tight and provide an abatement efficiency of at least 95% by weight POC, except as allowed by Regulation 8-5-328.1.2 fo r tank degassing.  The owner/operator is also subject to 
recordkeeping (Regulation 8-5-501).  Tesoro will be required to check the carbon canisters fo r breakthrough every other day.  Carbon Canisters A-1001 
and A-1002 will be used to abate three portable tanks , S-1491, and S-1490 and S-1491 (Application Number 9259).  
Each tank can emit 9.55 lb/day of POC.  As a conserva tive estimate, each pound of carbon can absorb one third of 
its weight with POC.  For a conservative estimate, assume  the canisters are abating all three tanks at one time. 
 3 tanks X 9.55 lb/day POC = 28.65 lb/day POC X 3 lb carbon/lb POC = 85.95 lb carbon/day 200 lb carbon bed/85.95 lb carbon/day = 2.32 days  The project is considered to be ministerial under the District's CEQA regulation 2-1-311 and therefore is not subject to CEQA review.  The engineering review for this pr oject requires only the application of standard permit 
conditions and standard emissions factors and therefore is not discretionary as defined by CEQA.  (Permit 
Handbook Chapter 4.1)  The project is over 1000 feet from the nearest school and therefore not subject to the public notification requirements of Reg. 2-1-412.
