BACKGROUND
Tesoro Refining and Marketing Company is applying for a modification to the Permit to Operate the following 
equipment:  
S-622 Fixed Cone Roof Tank; Tank A-622, Capacity:  3,360K Gallons, Storing:  Mixture of Diesel and 
Kerosene 
 
In the District database, S-622 is described incorrectly as shown:  
S-622 Internal Floating Roof Tank; Tank A-622, Capacity:  3,360K Gallons, Multi-liquid Tank 
 
The Federal Title V permit describes S-622 as a fixe d roof tank but with the incorrect tank contents:  
S-622 Fixed Roof Tank; Tank A-622, Capacity:  3,360K Gallons, Stori ng:  Distillate Oil, Gasoline 
 
Tesoro would like to correct the information in the District database and the Federal Title V permit to reflect the 
correct tank description AND contents.  S-622 Tank A-622 ha s been in service since 1951 and has a grandfathered 
throughput limit of 14600K barrels per year.  The tank is not being physically modified.  Updating the District database and Title V Permit to reflect the correct tank description and contents will not affect emissions.  S-622 Tank A-622 holds a mixture of diesel and kerosene from the two crude units.  The total vapor pressure for the 
contents is 0.42 psia.  The diesel and kerosene mixture has a gravity of 38 API.BACKGROUND
