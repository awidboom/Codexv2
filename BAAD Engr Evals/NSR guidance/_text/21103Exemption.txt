Policy: Clarification of Permit Exemptions per Reg 2-1-103

Policy           This policy clarifies two aspects of the permit exemption in Regulation 2-1-
                 103.

                 • A source that emits pollutants other than criteria pollutants may qualify for
                   this exemption.
                 • A source that does qualify for a source-specific exemption may still qualify
                   for the exemption in Regulation 2-1-103.


Rationale        The intent of Regulation 2-1-103 is to provide a permit exemption for a
                 source which is not otherwise regulated by the District, as long as the source
                 is not a toxic or public nuisance concern.

                 Regulation 2-1-103 may apply even if a source does not meet its source-
                 specific exemption in Regulation 2-1, Sections 114 through 128.


Effective date   June 20, 1996


Definitions      The following is a list of associated definitions.

                    •   Criteria Pollutant – Nitrogen Oxides (NOx), Sulfur Dioxide (SO2),
                        Carbon Monoxide (CO), particulate matter (PM10), Precursor Organic
                        Compounds (POC), or Non-Precursor Organic Compounds (NPOC).
