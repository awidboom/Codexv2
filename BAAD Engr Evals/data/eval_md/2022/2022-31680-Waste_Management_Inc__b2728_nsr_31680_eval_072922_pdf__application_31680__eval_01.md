A)

particulate emissions from the project are greater than the toxic trigger level of 
0.26 lb/year. All PM 10/PM 2.5 emissions are considered diesel particulate 
emissions.  Therefore, this project requires a Health Risk Assessment.  
 
There have been no other projects for new or modified sources at the facility for 
the past five years.

600 ft.
