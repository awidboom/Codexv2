BACKGROUND
The Pittsburg Owner  LPV, LLC  has applied  for an Authority to Construct/Permit to Operate for 
the following equipment:  
 
S-75 Portable Prime Water Pump Diesel Engine  
Tier 4 Final  with integrated SCR ; Make: John Deere ; Model: 6068CI550 ;  
Model Year: 2024 
277 brake horsepower ( bhp), 1.61 Million British Thermal Units per hour 
(MMBtu/hr ) 
CARB Executive Order: U -R-004-0676 
 
S-75 will operate at various locations within the Pittsburg Owner site at 696 W 10th Street in 
Pittsburg. S -75 will drive a water pump that will be used to manage stormwater at the various 
locations within the facility boundary.  S-75 meet s the Environmental Protection Agency and 
California Air Resources Board (EPA/CARB) Tier 4 Final  Off-road standard s for compression 
ignited engines . The engine will burn commercially available California low sulfur diesel fuel. The 
sulfur content of the diesel fuel will not exceed 0.0015% by weight.   
 
Portable engines are non -road engines as defined by 40 CFR 10393.801 and 1068.30.  Section 
209(e)(1) of the Federal Clean Air Act does not allow states вАЬor political subdivisionsвАЭ to impose 
emission control on non -road engines. The exception is standards a nd other requirements imposed 
by the State of California necessary to achieve attainment of air pollution standards. The regulatory 
analysis in this application will take this into account.  
 
This evaluation report will discuss compliance with all applicable rules and regulations for the 
proposed project.
