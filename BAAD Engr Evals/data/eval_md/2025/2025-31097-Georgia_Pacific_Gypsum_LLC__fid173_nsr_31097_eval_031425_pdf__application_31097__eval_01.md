This plant produces agricultural gypsum, plaster and wallboard products. The facility receives 
gypsum rock ore via ships and stores it in a dome located on the property. Rock from the dome 
is fed into a crusher (S-93 or S-95), and it reduces in size from 4" to less than%". The crushed 
gypsum ore is conveyed to the main processing building, where it is further reduced in size using 
roller mills and then calcined to stucco. 
Gypsum Rock Crushers S-93 and S-95 are identical equipment, and they will be interchanged 
into the process when one undergoes repairs. The owner/operator shall not operate the two 
sources concurrently per operating permit condition #26164.2.
