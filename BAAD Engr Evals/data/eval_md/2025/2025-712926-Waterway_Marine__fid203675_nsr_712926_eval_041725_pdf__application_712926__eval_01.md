## offsets
JSONPath: `$.offsets.narrative`

Offsets
ts  
 
Emission offset requirements for POC, and NO x are outlined in Regulation 2, Rule 2, 
Section 302.  POC and NO x offsets are required for new or modified sources at a facility 
that emits or will be permitted to emit 10 tons per year or more of that pollutant. 
Regulation 2 -2-303 requires offsets for any new or modified source at a major facility 
with a potential to e mit 100 tons per year or more of PM 10, PM 2.5, or sulfur dioxide (SO 2).  
Since the facility  will not exceed 10 tons per year of POC and NOx and 100 tons per year 
of PM 10, PM 2.5 and SO 2,, offsets are not required.
