manufacturer.  It is assumed that the abatement efficiency for POC is 50% by weight. The engine 
will operate for emergencies and will be limited to a maximum of 50 hours per yea r for 
maintenance and testing.  
   
This evaluation report will discuss compliance of the proposed project with all applicable rules 
and regulations.

This engine will be operated at a hazardous air pollutant (HAP) area source. Therefore, the

ZZZZ pursuant to 40 CFR Part 63.6590(c). Therefore, S -1 complies with the NESHAP by 
meeting the requirements under 40 CFR Part 60, Subpart JJJJ.
