compounds (POC) from unburned diesel fuel, sulfur dioxide (SO 2) and particulate matter 
(PM 10).  All of these pollutants are briefly discussed on the District’s website at 
www.baaqmd.gov .

(EPA/CARB) Tier 4 Off-road standard.  The engine will burn commercially available California 
low-sulfur diesel fuel.  The sulfur content of the diesel fuel will not exceed 0.0015% by weight.      
   
This evaluation report will discuss compliance of the proposed project with all applicable rules 
and regulations.
