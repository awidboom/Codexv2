Condition Number #  24173  
PHASE II EXEMPTION PER REG.8 -7-112.9, ORVR FLEETS  
1. All vehicles and equipment refueled at this facility shall be owned by a common operator and

 5. All remaining vapor recovery piping (including internal dispenser piping) shall be capped
