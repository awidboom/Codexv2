The Home Depot #6677  is applying for an Authority to Construct  for the following

Make: Volvo Penta , Model: TAD1371VE , Model Year: 2023  
388 bhp, 2.56 MMBtu/hr  
Permit Condition No s. 100072 and 100073  
 
S-2 is replacing S -1 at the facility.  The criteria pollutants are nitrogen oxides (NOx), 
carbon monoxide (CO), precursor organic compounds (POC) from unburned diesel fuel, 
sulfur dioxide (SO 2) and particulate matter (PM 10).  All of these pollutants are briefly 
discussed on the District’s web site at www.baaqmd.gov . 
 
S-2 meets  the Environmental Protection Agency and California Air Resources Board 
(EPA/CARB) Tier 4 Off-road standard.  The engine  will burn commercially available 
California low sulfur diesel fuel.  The sulfur content of the diesel fuel will not exceed 
0.0015% by weight.   
 
This evaluation report will discuss compliance of the proposed project with all applicable 
rules and regulations.
