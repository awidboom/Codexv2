BACKGROUND
OUND  
 
The applicant has requested  an Authority to Construct and Permit to Operate for the following:  
 
S-1 Gasoline Dispensing Facility (GDF)  
 
The facility configurations are described below:  
 
Proposed Construction  
3 gasoline UST s (3 – 40,000 gallon ) 
Phase I OPW EVR (VR -102) 
Assist EVR System with ARID Permeator and Veeder -Root ISD (VR -202) 
1 gasoline addative UST (1 – 1,500 gallon)  
30 double  product gasoline nozzles  
36 million gallons of gasoline per year throughput limi t 
 
This is a new GDF with no historical throughput.  
 
The applicant requested a gasoline throughput of 36.0 million gallons per year. This analysis estimates the 
incremental health risk resulting from toxic air contaminant (TAC) emissions from gasoline dispensing 
facility at requested throughput. Results from  this HRA indicate that the project cancer risk is estimated at 
2.7 million, the project chronic hazard index is estimated at 0.0 41, and the project acute hazard index is 
estimated at 0. 45. In accordance with the District’s Regulation 2, Rule 5, this propo sed project compl ies 
with Regulation 2 -5-302 (Project Risk Requirement) because the project cancer risk does not exceed 6.0 
in a million. Since this project is located in an Overburdened Community, as defined in Regulation 2 -1-
243, it is subject to a cancer risk standard of 6.0 in a million, chronic and acute hazard index standards of 
1.0, pursuant to District’s Regulation 2 -5-302 (Project Risk Requirement).  
 
This application is being processed as a new source defined in Regulation 2 -1-232.
