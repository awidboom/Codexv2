BACKGROUND
ND  
 
Alameda County  – General Services Agency (GSA)  has applied to obtain an Authority to 
Construct and a Permit to Operate for the following equipment:  
 
S-5 Emergency Standby Diesel Fire Pump  Engine  
Engine Manufacturer: John Deere , Model: 4045 , Model Year: 202 3 
Engine Family: PJDXL04 .5119  
140 BHP, 1.71 MMBTU/hr  
Displacement: 4.5 L ( 275 cu. in.)  
4 In-line Cylinders  
Permit Condition Nos. 100072, 100073 , 10076  
 
The proposed S-5 emergency standby diesel fire pump engine will be  installed at the Alameda 
County GSA  headquarters  located at 1401 Lakeside Drive, Oakland, CA  and effectively 
replace the existing S -2 emergency standby  diesel  fire pump engine . S-5 will help provide 
power to the building fire system during any emergency situations such as unexpected power 
outages.   
 
S-5 meets  the Environmental Protection Agency and California Air Resources Board 
(EPA/CARB) Tier 3 Final  Off-road standard. The engine  will burn commercially available 
California low sulfur diesel fuel. The sulfur content of the diesel fuel will not exceed 
0.0015% by weight.
