This project involves removing all the single wall USTS and installing 2 new double wall split USTs, 
including 1 -20K gallon split UST (1 -12K gal. gasoline and 1 -8K gal. gasoline) and 1 -20K gallon split UST 
(1-12K gal. biodiesel and 1 -8K gal. E85), including rem oving the 8 triple product gasoline nozzles and 
installing 8 dual product nozzles, 8 biodiesel nozzles, and 8 E85 nozzles.  
 
The addition of the annual 75,000 gallons throughput of E85 will result in a slight increase in POC 
emissions and the annual benzene emissions but does not meet the trigger for the HRA requirement.  
 
This application is being processed as a modification as defined in Regulation 2 -1-234.
