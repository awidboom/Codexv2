assuming the use of mechanical shoe prim ary seals therefore there will not be an

 This will be considered an Administrative Am endment for purposes of Title V since the 
changes are merely clarifying the tank seal de scriptions.  Only section VI of the Title V 
permit will be corrected to reflect the accura te descriptions of these tank seals.
