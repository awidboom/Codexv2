BACKGROUND
The Tesoro Refining and Marketing Company (Tesoro) was issued the Authority to Construct to modify the 
following equipment at the Golden Eagle Refinery in Martinez, California: 
 
S-920 No. 2 HDS Charge Heater, No. 20 Furnace, Foster Wheeler, Maximum Firing Rate:  63 MMBtu/hr  S-1001 No. 50 Crude Unit  S-1003 No. 2 HDS Unit  
The modifications are needed in orde r to produce ultra low sulfur diesel (ULSD) to comply with CARB and EPA 
clean diesel requirements.  Tesoro must  decrease the total sulfur content of th e diesel from the current concentration 
of 100-150 wppm to 15 wppm.  The use of ULSD fuel in mo tor vehicles is required by June 1, 2006.  Tesoro will 
begin making ULSD in 2006.   See the engineering evaluation in Application 9788 for a detailed discussion of process changes and emission calculations.  Emissions from the ULSD project ar e all fugitive precursor organic co mpounds.  In the fugitive component 
emission factors, Tesoro supplied the incorrect factor for Connectors and Flanges.  The emission factor supplied 
was in the units of lb/day/source instead of lb/hr/source.  The emission factors used in the application were originally developed for the original CARB Phase 3 Clean  Fuels Project (Application Number 2508).  The emission 
factor for Connectors and Flanges was verified in the engineering evaluation for the CARB Phase 3 Project and the factor of 0.004 is in the units of lb/day/source  versus lb/hr/source.  The emission factor of 0.004 lb/day/source is 
equivalent to 0.000167 lb/hr/source.  Th e emissions factors for all other f ugitive components used in the ULSD 
Project were verified with the CARB Ph ase 3 Project and are correct.   The corrected emission calculation is shown 
in the table below.
