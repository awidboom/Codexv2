Except for SO2, the emission factors for these engines are from the CARB Certification (CARB Executive Order # U-R-002-0214-2).  The SO2 emissions were calculated based on 
the maximum allowable sulfur content (0.05 wt% S) of the diesel fuel with the assumption that all of the sulfur present will be convert ed to SO2 during the combustion process.  The
