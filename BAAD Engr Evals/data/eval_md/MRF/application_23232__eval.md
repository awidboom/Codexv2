BACKGROUND
The Tesoro Refining and Marketing Company (Tesoro) is applying for an Administrative 
Change in Conditions to their Permit to Operate to add the requirements of 40 CFR 64, 
Compliance Assu rance Monitoring (CAM), for the following equipment:  
 
S-963 Alkylation Plant Gas Turbine 177, Natural Gas Fired, 113MM Btu/hr  
 
The CAM requirements for S -963 will be satisfied with fuel gas and steam monitoring already in 
place.  40 CFR 64.6(c) lists the minimum requirements that are required to be included in 
permit conditions.  The requirements of 40 CFR 64 are listed in the table appended at the 
end of this engineering evaluation.BACKGROUND
