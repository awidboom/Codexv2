3.0 Cumulative Increase  
There is no increase in emissions as a result of this application; therefore, cumulative increase does not apply. This 
application does not involve any physical changes  other than the installation of CEMS for monitoring purposes , 
changes in the method of operation, or increase in any firing rates that would  result in an increase in either the 
daily or annual emissions of any regulated air pollutant. This application is a change in conditions for the way 
Marathon demonstrates overall emissions compliance with Regulation 9 -10 through mass emissions (lbs/day) 
instead of an activity -based emission factor (lbs/MMBtu).  
 
4.0 Toxic Screening Analysis  
There i s no increase in emissions of toxic air contaminants as a result of this application; therefore, a health risk 
assessment is not triggered  nor required per Regulation 2, Rule 5.

6.0 Offsets  
There is no increase in emissions as a result of this application; therefore, offsets are not required per Regulation 
2-2-302 and Regulation 2-2- 303. Per Regulation 9 -10-308.1 , NO x ERCs generated in accordance with Regulation 2, 
Rule 2 may be surrendered on a one -time basis at a 1.15 to 1 ratio to offset all or part of the NO x emissions 
reductions required, and the daily NO x emissions limit will be adjusted accordingly. Based on the  proposed daily 
NO x mass limit as established in Table 2, the facility has agreed to  surrender NO x ERCs  on a one -time basis to make 
up the differenc e between the actual operating emissions in lbs/day of NO x, which are unchanged by this 
application, and the emissions that would meet the 0.033 lbs/MMBtu of NO x limit in Regulation 9 -10-301.
