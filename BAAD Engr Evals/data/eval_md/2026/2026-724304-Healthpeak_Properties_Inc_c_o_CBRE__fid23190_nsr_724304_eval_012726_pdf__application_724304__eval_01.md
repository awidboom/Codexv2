Background
ound    
Healthpeak  Properties, Inc. c/o CBRE  is applying for an Authority to Construct/Permit to 
Operate for the following equipment:    
   
S-2 Emergency  Use Diesel Back -Up Engine  
Make: AB Volvo Penta , Model: TWD1672GE,  Model Year: 2025   
836 bhp, 5.27MMBtu/hr     
Permit Condition Nos. 100072 , 100073 , and 1008 27  
with integral Selective Catalytic Reduction  
 
The criteria pollutants are nitrogen oxides (NOx), carbon monoxide (CO), precursor organic 
compounds (POC) from unburned diesel fuel, sulfur dioxide (SO 2) and particulate matter 
(PM 10).  All of these pollutants are briefly discussed on the Air District’s website at 
www.baaqmd.gov .   
  
S-2 meets the Environmental Protection Agency and California Air Resources Board 
(EPA/CARB) Tier 4 Off-road standard.  The engine will burn commercially available California 
low-sulfur diesel fuel.  The sulfur content of the diesel fuel will not exceed 0.0015% by weight.      
   
This evaluation report will discuss compliance of the proposed project with all applicable rules 
and regulations.
