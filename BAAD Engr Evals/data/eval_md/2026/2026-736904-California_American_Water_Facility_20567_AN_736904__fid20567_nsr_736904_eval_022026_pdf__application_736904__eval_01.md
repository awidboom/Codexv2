BACKGROUND
California American Water  has applied for an Authority to Construct for the following 
equipment:  
 
S-2 Diesel fired Emergency engine – Generator  
Make: AB Volvo Penta, Model: VD250 -04FT4, EPA Family: SVPXL12.8CJB  
388 bhp; 2.95 MMBtu/hour (21.5 gal/hour); Tier 4, Year: 2025  
 
Permit Condition Nos. 100072 and 100073  
 
The source replaces : 
 
S-1 Diesel fired Emergency engine  
179 bhp, Engine Make: Caterpillar , Model:  3304 , Year: 1999 
 
This emergency  engine will be  located at 5040 Old Redwood Hwy, Santa Rosa, CA 95403  
and will replace  an existing permitted engine , S-1. The facility states  that engine, S -1, is 
planned for decommissioning in early 2026.  
 
The criteria pollutants are nitrogen oxides (NOx), carbon monoxide (CO), precursor 
organic compounds (POC) from unburned diesel fuel, sulfur dioxide (SO 2) and particulate 
matter (PM 10).  All of these pollutants are briefly discussed on the Air District ’s web site 
at www.baaqmd.gov . 
 
S-2 meets the Environmental Protection Agency and California Air Resources Board 
(EPA/CARB) Tier 4 Off-road standard.  The engine will burn commercially available 
California low sulfur diesel fuel.  The sulfur content of the diesel fuel will not exceed 
0.0015% by weight.  
 
This evaluation report will discuss compliance of the proposed project with all applicable 
rules and regulations.
