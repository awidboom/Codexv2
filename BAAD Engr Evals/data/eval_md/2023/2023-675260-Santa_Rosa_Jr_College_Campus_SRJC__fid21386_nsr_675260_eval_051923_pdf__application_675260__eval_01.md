The equipment will operate at 680 Sonoma Mountain Parkway, Petaluma, CA, t his is not 
considered an Overburdened Community (OBC), as defined in Regulation 2 -1-243. 
 
The criteria pollutants are nitrogen oxides (NOx), carbon monoxide (CO), precursor 
organic compounds (POC) from unburned diesel fuel, sulfur dioxide (SO 2) and particulate 
matter (PM 10).  All of these pollutants are briefly discussed on the District’s web site at 
www.baaqmd.gov . 
 
S-3 meet s the Environmental Protection Agency and California Air Resources Board 
(EPA/CARB) Tier 3 Off-road standard.  The engine will burn commercially available 
California low sulfur diesel fuel.  The sulfur content of the diesel fuel will not exceed 
0.0015% by weight . 
 
This evaluation report will discuss compliance of the proposed project with all a pplicable 
rules and regulations.
