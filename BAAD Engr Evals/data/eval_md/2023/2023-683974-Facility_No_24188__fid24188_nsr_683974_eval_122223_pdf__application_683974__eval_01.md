Lyten  is proposing to install and operate 36 carbon reactors  and a solvent cleaning operation  at a 
pilot plant facility  located at 145 Baytech San Jose, CA, 95134 . The reactors will be periodically 
cleaned by a solvent cleaning operation consisting of 50 gallons of acetone usage per year. T he 
36 reactors  will use thermal or microwave -based energy , to react methane  within natural  gas into 
carbon -based products.  The reactors will  also be fed  nitrogen and argon , while the gases will be 
treated with electrical current.   
 
Immediately downstream of each reactor is a cyclone, filter, condenser and then adsorber, in that 
order. The cyclone and filter are each responsible for separating out a specific product , and act as 
abatement devices for specific pollutants.  Further downstream, t he condenser followed by  
adsorber  function as abatement devices for each reactor.

yielded  non-detect  result s, below the concentration threshold needed for a reading. In these 
cases, the value that was used was one half the lowest measurable concentration of the test.
