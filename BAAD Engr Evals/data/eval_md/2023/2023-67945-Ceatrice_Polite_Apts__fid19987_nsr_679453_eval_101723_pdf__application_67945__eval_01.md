and consists of an approximately 0.358 -acre tract of land developed with a nine -story apartment 
complex  occupied by the Ceatrice Polite Apartments. The first floor of t he apartment complex

Depressurization  System (S SD) to reduce the potential of i ntrusion of soil gas into the indoor air 
of the onsite apartment building. The SSD system will include a  195 cfm vacuum blower without 
any abatement .  
 
The main compound found in the soil was isopropanol. Emissions calculations  were  based on 
laboratory results from samples taken  at the site . No emissions are expected to trigger Health Risk 
Assessment per  Regulation 2 -5, Table 1 .

concentrations will  be monitored with a flame -ionization detector (FID).
